Why SaniBook matters

Sanitation is often treated as an afterthought. SaniBook reframes the problem as systems and people work — showing how small, well-directed interventions prevent disease, improve dignity, and unlock economic value for cities and communities.

Who this book is for

  • City planners and local government officials
  • NGO implementers and field officers
  • Consultants, students and researchers working on WASH

What you'll get

  • Simple decision trees for choosing sanitation solutions
  • Budget templates and procurement checklists
  • Monitoring indicators and sample KPIs
  • Community engagement playbook

Inside the book
