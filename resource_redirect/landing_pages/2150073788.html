Today, The Amos Youth Centre (AYC), is a community-led and youth-driven organization, that focuses on creating quality, inclusive opportunities for learning, health, entrepreneurship, and leadership development among promising Zambian youth and women. By 2034, the goal is to generate 500,000 meaningful experiences annually for children, youth, and women.

AYC envisions creating a self-sustaining Learning & Leadership Center where 1,500 participants join programs 3-5 times weekly. Additionally, there are plans to set up ten hubs/outreach posts across Kafue, positively affecting 500 youth, particularly in rural areas. The overarching goal is to shape a future where every part of the community experiences empowerment and opportunity.
