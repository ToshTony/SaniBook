Why the SaniBook Matters

Transforming Kenya’s fragmented sanitation landscape into a unified, data-driven engine of sustainable impact.

The Imperative for the SaniBook

The Kenyan sanitation sector faces a fundamental challenge — systemic data fragmentation. Vital operational data is scattered across numerous, non-standardized silos among government, NGOs, and private players. This fractured information landscape causes redundant resource allocation, duplicated efforts, and a major disconnect between grassroots interventions and national policy frameworks.

“Without shared data, progress remains invisible — and invisible progress cannot be sustained.”

The SaniBook is designed to resolve this by creating a unified and standardized data ecosystem. It bridges the gap between on-the-ground action and high-level strategy through real-time sector visibility — enabling coordinated decision-making, resource efficiency, and robust policymaking across Kenya.

Core Function and Value

The SaniBook serves as the “Sanitation Nexus” — a centralized platform that transforms fragmented organizational data into a powerful, coherent national asset.

Evidence & Advocacy

Builds a unified evidence base that shifts advocacy from anecdotal to data-driven—fueling smarter funding and informed policymaking.

Standardization & Professionalism

Democratizes knowledge and sets sector-wide standards, helping grassroots actors gain national recognition and credibility.

Credibility & Coordination

Acts as a strategic compass — enhancing coordination, credibility, and visibility among investors and policymakers.

The Sanibook: The Future Starts Here

The SaniBook is not just a registry — it’s the foundational digital infrastructure that ensures local action translates into sustainable national impact. Together, we can build Kenya’s sanitation future on a foundation of shared knowledge, data integrity, and unified purpose.
