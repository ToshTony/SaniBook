Long Term Impact


Through this funding opportunity, the Cisco Foundation supported FUNDES Catalyst's venture studio "EcoBodegas," placing healthy farm-sourced food directly on community shelves. Simultaneously, it empowered small retailers to innovate and compete with larger brands.

EcoBodegas has revolutionized micro-businesses and small production plants with inadequate infrastructure. Formerly limited in product variety, facing suboptimal packaging and a lack of network and client base due to new market entry, these sole distribution channels now benefit from EcoBodegas' white label branding, improved packaging, and access to distribution channels across traditional retail. A percentage of these small local¬†agro-ecological producers have even joined the National Association of Ecological and Fair Trade Producers.

¬†



The impact venture studio in Latin America strategically employs accessible technology to address daily challenges faced by micro-businesses and their ecosystems. It establishes social enterprises and tech solutions, unlocking the potential of micro-businesses to combat poverty. FUNDES Catalyst, while embracing entrepreneurship as a catalyst for sustainable growth, advocates for scalable business models that perpetuate a lasting impact.

Looking ahead, FUNDES Catalyst ambitiously aims to equip millions (1M by 2026) of subsistence entrepreneurs with tools to thrive, not just survive. Aligned with the goals of job creation and fostering community-driven change, FUNDES Catalyst leverages its ability to aggregate needs, demand, and supply for social enterprises lacking access to market, capital, and education. This support overcomes barriers and facilitates growth, enabling these enterprises to generate livelihoods for their communities.
